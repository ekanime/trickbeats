ABOUT

Born and raised in Southern Nigeria,Trickbeats grew up listening to old school hip hop and afro music which made him develop an interest in audio production.

Over the years, the sounds have evolved in conjunction with eras of music from the origins where classic jazz, soul and highlife were sampled and transformed into the most heard urban sounds in recent years. Trickbeats have evolved in the same manner to adapt to the most demanded styles of making music.He boasts a collection of beat tapes.

Trickbeats has leveraged the adaptations of digital music to combine elements of morden day hip-hop and Afro style drumming, soulful chords and funky progressions using synths to create his own unique style.

His primary tool of the trade is the piano and when he does his thing on the keys you will know he has been making music for a long time.
